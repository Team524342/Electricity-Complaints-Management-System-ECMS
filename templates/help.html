Help & Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a complaint?

To submit a complaint:

  1. Login to your account
  2. Click on "Submit Complaint" in the navigation menu
  3. Fill out the complaint form with all required details
  4. Upload any supporting documents if needed
  5. Click the "Submit" button

You will receive a confirmation once your complaint is successfully submitted.

How can I track the status of my complaint?

You can track the status of your complaint by:

  1. Login to your account
  2. Navigate to your dashboard
  3. View the list of your complaints
  4. Click on "View Details" for the specific complaint you want to track

The status can be one of the following:

  • Open: Your complaint has been received but not yet processed
  • In Progress: Your complaint is being addressed by our team
  • Resolved: Your complaint has been resolved
What if I forgot my password?

If you forgot your password:

  1. Click on the "Login" link
  2. Click on "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter your registered email address
  4. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password

If you continue to have issues, please contact our support team.

How long does it take to resolve a complaint?

The time taken to resolve a complaint depends on its nature and complexity:

  • Simple complaints such as billing queries may be resolved within 24-48 hours
  • Technical issues like voltage problems may take 2-3 days
  • Complex infrastructure issues might take up to a week or more

Rest assured, our team works diligently to address every complaint as quickly as possible.

Can I update my complaint after submission?

While you cannot directly edit a complaint after submission, you can:

  1. Submit a new complaint with updated information
  2. Include the reference number of your original complaint
  3. Mention that this is an update to a previous complaint

Our team will link the new information to your original complaint.
